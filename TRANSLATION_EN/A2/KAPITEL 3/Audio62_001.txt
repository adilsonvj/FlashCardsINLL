- I really like making music and playing the clarinet in the village band.
- The band performs 2000.
- We rehearse together, play concerts, and march through the streets with the antiphon/hymn to the Hämmelsmarsch.
- I like to volunteer in my association.
- As president, I have trouble motivating the youth of today to join the association.
- They prefer other kinds of music or they rather stay at home.
- We all do a lot of preparation to make the band more attractive for young people.
