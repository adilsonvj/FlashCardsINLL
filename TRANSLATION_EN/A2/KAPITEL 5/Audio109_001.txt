- Luxembourg, country of the castles and the boys.
- Boys, castles and fortress ruins belong to the most important attractions of Luxembourg.
- There are in total 76 castles and fortresses or on Manns Truiden there of them.
- For a few decades the state has started to buy up the most important buildings, in order to make them accessible to tourists from home and abroad.
- The most beautifully renovated castles are in Feianen, Befort, Burschent, Klef and in the rock.
- The beautiful ruins can be admired in Didling, Dürlem, Ersauer, Hesper, Kersch, Mersch and Usselding.
- There are cultural activities in these castles and fortresses.
- Concerts, art exhibitions, conferences, music festivals and medieval festivals.
- The best known is probably the Family of Man photograph exhibit by Edward Steichen at the castle in Klef and the medieval festival, which always takes place in the summer at the castle in Feianen.
- People, who wander, can do the national triple-town tour from the valley of the Siebeschlässer.
- This is the route of 37 kilometers, from Geischel at the Belger border to Mersch.
- The tour goes through Böscher, Felder and Dierfer, always following the river.
- And in the end, you can see far castles of Kärsch, Zimmer, Ernseburg, Heisender 2, Hölmes, Schöndes and Mersch.
