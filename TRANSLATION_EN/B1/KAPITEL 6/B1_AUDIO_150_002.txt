- National anthem.
- The national anthem of Luxembourg is the first and last stanza of the song "Ons Heemecht" from 1895.
- The text is by Michel Lentz and the music by Jean-Antoine Zinnen.
- In the first stanza, the beauty of the Luxembourgish landscape is described.
- In the last stanza it is about our freedom and independence, which the country has enjoyed since 1867.
