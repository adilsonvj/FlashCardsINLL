- Luxembourg is a parliamentary democracy in the form of a constitutional monarchy.
- That means the people democratically elect a parliament.
- And that the head of state is a monarch, and not a president as in a republic.
- Since 2000, Grand Duke Henri of the House of Nassau-Weilburg has been the head of state.
- His wife is named Maria Teresa.
- They have five children, four boys and one girl.
- The eldest son is the heir apparent, Guillaume.
- He has been married since 2012 to Countess Stéphanie de Lannoy.
