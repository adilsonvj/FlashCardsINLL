- Symbols for Luxembourg.
- The flag.
- The Luxembourg flag in red, white, and blue.
- But there is also a second one, that is also displayed at sports events.
- One is blue and white striped, with a red lion in the middle.
- In the background there is the state coat of arms, which is now displayed as a flag.
- In Luxembourg, as a member state of the European Union, the European flag is naturally also always shown, the twelve stars on a blue background.
