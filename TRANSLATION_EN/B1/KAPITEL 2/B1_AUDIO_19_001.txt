- I have a subscription for the theater.
- I put together the program myself, and therefore it is immensely varied.
- From small productions to operas to ballet, I like everything.
- Prices, especially such a subscription, are also quite social, and the advantage is that you always have a good seat.
- Unfortunately, I often have to go alone.
- My friend does not have a subscription and does not always get a seat.
- If there is a show in the week on the program, it is naturally sometimes exhausting to sit in the theater for more than three hours after a long day of work and get up early again in the morning.
