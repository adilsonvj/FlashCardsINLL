- The Hieflesche north is often such a southern part of the Ardennes,
- from which then a strong contrast to the flat Gutland.
- Even though most people live there, these idyllic counterpoints attract athletes and tourists as well,
- who come to see the many castles and forts.
- The north of our country is simply the ideal place,
- to take a breath of fresh air for a while,
- to switch off and actively experience nature.
