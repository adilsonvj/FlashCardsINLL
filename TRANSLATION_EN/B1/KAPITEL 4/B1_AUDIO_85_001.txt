- The Schafeleb of the Post, next to the state as a public establishment, the largest patron, thus employer, in Luxembourg.
- The Post employs more than 4,000 people from more than 40 nationalities.
- The Post offers a wide range of services.
- It provides Post Services for mailing letters and parcels and distributing daily newspapers, as well as financial services and, not to be forgotten, services in the telecommunications sector.
- Additionally, there are also special services for people who collect stamps.
- The Post issues about 30 new stamps and postcards every year.
