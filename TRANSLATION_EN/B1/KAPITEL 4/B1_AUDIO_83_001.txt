- Can one work as a French teacher in a municipal school?
- The teaching in Luxembourg is for the most part state-run, but there are also a few private schools.
- Not everyone who works for a public school is, therefore, a civil servant.
- About 10,000 teachers work in primary and secondary education.
- In primary school there are many more women than men.
- Naturally, there is also education for adults in Luxembourg, such as the CNLPA, ENAD, or even the National Language Institute ENAL, where one can learn a language.
- Additionally, Luxembourg got a university in 2003, which has since earned an international good reputation.
