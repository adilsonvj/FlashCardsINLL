- A large part of occupational diseases are skin diseases.
- That can for example be eczema with red blisters or ruptures that hurt.
- Often people affected are those who work in industry and deal with aggressive chemicals.
- Through contact with these chemicals allergic reactions can develop.
- But also the constant contact with water or soap irritates the skin.
- Therefore the risk is relatively high for hairdressers or buttocks staff.
- Preventively one should wash hands and let the skin dry well.
