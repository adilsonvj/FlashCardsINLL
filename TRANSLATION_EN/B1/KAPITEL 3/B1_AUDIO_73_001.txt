- What kinds of illnesses are particularly common among the different professions?
- Look at Columns 1 and 2 and take a break.
- Listen to the texts again once and check.
- In many workplaces there are specific dust or chemical substances in the air that can be breathed in.
- That can lead to inflammations, allergies, or even asthma.
- The risk professions include, for example, bakers, painters, hairdressers, but also people who deal with plants and dust.
- It is important that good ventilation is provided in these workplaces.
- One can also wear a respirator mask that does not inhale too much dust.
