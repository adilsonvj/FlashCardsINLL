- At many workplaces there is specific dust or chemical substance in the air that one can inhale.
- That can lead to inflammation, allergies, or even asthma.
- The risk groups include bakers, painters, hairdressers, and also people who deal with plants and animals.
- It is important that these workplaces are well ventilated.
- One can also put on a breathing mask to avoid inhaling too much dust.
