- The back pain sheets are those of the muscles and the skeleton.
- Many occupational groups are affected by this.
- On the one hand, it can be people who have to work physically hard,
- but on the other hand also people who take the same position for a long time.
- Heavy lifting strains the spine.
- Sitting in front of the computer for a long time can lead to back pain or tension in the neck.
- In order for that not to happen, one should change position often.
- Additionally, one can strengthen their musculature through strength training.
- It can also help when one works at the desk in an upright position.
- In any case, it is important to always sit/stand correctly, no matter what one does.
