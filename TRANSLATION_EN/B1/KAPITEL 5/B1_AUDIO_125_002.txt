- Housing cooperatives.
- In the concept of the housing cooperative or cooperative, important values include sociability, sustainability, and affordable prices.
- Members of a cooperative commit themselves to respecting these values.
- It may be participatively organized, where citizens can collectively plan, create, and act on their living spaces.
- Social diversity is promoted, generations live together, and this creates group dynamics.
- The individual members of the cooperative buy shares in the project and can live there for their cheapest rent.
