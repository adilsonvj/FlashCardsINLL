- Around it, from Valencia, about the alternative housing construction.
- Higher up, the crowded Mr. Lukawa Konzer, responsible for ProRéveil, followed.
- As is known, there should be a housing shelter on the Ällefabriksterräng for about 2000 people.
- Our office was directly interested.
- We have been working in the ecological and socially minded field for a long time.
- And that was really an opportunity to realize many of the next things.
- We have now drafted a project for 375 houses and 375 apartments on a 27-hectare site.
- That is as large as 38 football fields.
- Important for us was that cars are not allowed to drive everywhere in the shelter.
- We rely on gentle mobility, meaning any non-motorized means of transport, i.e., bicycles, scooters, those on foot.
- We also support car sharing.
- And now in the parking lot outside, of course, also space for electric cars to be charged.
- To ensure mobility, of course the shelter must be well connected to public transport.
- It is not far away, there are three bus lines that sometimes run.
- And since last month there is also a tram stop here at 300 meters.
- Regarding electricity, we rely on renewable energies.
- Each house has solar panels.
- In addition, we are supplied with electricity from the windmills in the north of the shelter.
- When we speak ecologically, we must not forget recycling.
- It is clear that there will be containers in some places for paper, glass, and plastic to recycle.
- Each house has its own special bins for compost, which can be used in the community garden.
- By the way, community garden.
- The garden is divided into small plots that one can rent.
- Anyone who works there can use the shared infrastructures and naturally also benefit from the harvest.
- It is a place where many social contacts arise, and where not only garden hobbyists meet.
- It was also important for us that the shelter is very green.
- The apartments and residences themselves do not have large plots with big gardens, but they have access to common green areas.
- It is also important that there are small shops in the shelter, so one does not have to drive to the big supermarket for every little thing.
- This promotes local commerce, and you do not have to take the car or bus for everything.
- In the center of the shelter there is a meeting point, a kind of hall, which can be used for various purposes, such as cultural events.
- Here there is also a small café where people like to meet.
- Next to the garden and playground, that is the area where most social contacts arise.
- All in all, people seem to be very satisfied with it.
