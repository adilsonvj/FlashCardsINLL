- When our neighbor who came to the nursing home two years ago because she can no longer live at home is,
- my husband and I told ourselves that we did not want that in the long run, so living in a nursing home.
- We want to stay in our own home for as long as possible.
- Therefore we have wondered and stumbled upon a very nice project.
- Not far from us there is a housing community exclusively for elderly people.
- They do not live together in one apartment, but in a residence where everyone has a small apartment and can live autonomously.
- The apartments are all age-appropriate.
- There are also a few common rooms where people can meet.
- In addition, there is a network connected where one can get help on the spot and also receive medical care.
- We have registered to get a housing unit there,
- but unfortunately we are already on a waiting list for months.
