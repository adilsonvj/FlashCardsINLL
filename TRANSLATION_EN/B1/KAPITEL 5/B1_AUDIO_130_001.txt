- We took Hakim in three months ago at our place.
- He is 35 years old and comes from a war zone.
- At first he lived in a foyer.
- Until he has an official status, he may not work in Luxembourg.
- That is very hard for him.
- Now he lives with us and helps a lot around the house.
- He also often watches the children or takes them to school.
- For us, that is a big relief.
- It is also nice to get to know a person with a different cultural background a bit better.
- That helps a lot to understand his situation better.
- Sometimes we notice that I, too, would like to be with my family alone sometimes, even though Hakim is with our family.
- We still have problems understanding each other, because Hakim's language skills are not that good yet.
- We have also learned some Arabic.
