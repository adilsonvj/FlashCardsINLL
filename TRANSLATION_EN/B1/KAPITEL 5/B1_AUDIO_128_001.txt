- I wanted for a long time to buy my apartment in Luxembourg, but there was a big problem: I did not have enough money.
- The key was the Independent Conference of the Teieren Housing Cooperative.
- That is a concept where there is neither a real owner nor a normal tenant.
- I found that an incredibly cool idea and joined there.
- I bought shares and am now a member of a cooperative.
- I may also participate with money, which is important for living.
- Here it is democratically decided, and we manage the whole thing together.
- Aspects like ecological and sustainable living are respected.
- Financially it is also very interesting.
- The rent is not as high as in normal apartments,
- and I have a guarantee that I can stay in my apartment, even without owning a property.
- But I must say, it takes a lot of time,
- that there are many meetings and often long discussions in the demand of the cooperative.
