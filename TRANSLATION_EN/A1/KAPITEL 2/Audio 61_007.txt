- Sprangprozession.
- Pentecost Sunday and Pentecost Monday are holidays in Luxembourg.
- The day after, Pentecost Tuesday, there is the Sprangprozession in Echternach.
- It is a religious procession.
- People jump through the streets of Echternach up to the basilica.
- The Sprangprozession has been on the UNESCO list since November 2010.
