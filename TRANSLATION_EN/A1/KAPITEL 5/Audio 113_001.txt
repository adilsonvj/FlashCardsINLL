- Read the dialogue aloud first.
- Goodbye.
- What do you do when I knock?
- I would like to make orange juice.
- I really like oranges from Spain.
- They are very juicy, but perfect for making juice.
- Give me two kilos when I knock.
- Anything else?
- Yes, I also get peaches.
- How many?
- Ten pieces.
- Anything else?
- Yes, I would like asparagus when I knock.
- Oh, I’m sorry. I don’t have any asparagus left.
- So, how much are the tomatoes?
- I have tomatoes from Spain for three euros and 49 cents per kilo.
- And I have organic tomatoes from Belgium for four euros and 75 cents per kilo.
- I want one kilo of organic tomatoes.
- Is that all?
- Yes, that’s everything.
- That comes to nine euros and 75 cents.
- Do you pay with cash or by card?
- Cash, here you go, ten euros.
- And here is your 25 cents change.
- Thank you. And have a nice day.
- Thank you as well. Bye.
