- Mr. Clemence works from Monday to Friday.
- He normally goes in the morning at eight o'clock or eight o'clock five.
- And he celebrates at five o'clock again home.
- He doesn't do much sport, but he goes swimming now and then.
- His wife works only half a day.
- Monday, Wednesday and Friday mornings, and Tuesday and Thursday after noon.
- She goes running often.
- And she plays tennis with a friend every Saturday.
